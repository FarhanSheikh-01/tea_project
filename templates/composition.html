Tea Composition Analysis

Introduction

Tea Composition Analysis is a comprehensive study of the chemical and physical properties of tea leaves, aimed at understanding their flavor, aroma, and health benefits. The project employs advanced techniques such as spectroscopy and machine learning to analyze the key components like polyphenols, caffeine, and amino acids in tea samples.

Objectives

  • Identify and quantify the key chemical compounds in tea leaves.
  • Develop a dataset for different tea types and their compositions.
  • Establish a correlation between chemical composition and quality grading.
  • Explore potential health benefits based on composition analysis.

Tools and Techniques

  • Spectroscopy: For analyzing chemical properties and composition.
  • High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC): For detecting polyphenols, caffeine, and amino acids.
  • Machine Learning: To build predictive models for tea grading and categorization.
  • Python and Libraries: Tools like NumPy, Pandas, and Scikit-learn for data processing and analysis.

Future Scope

The study of tea composition has vast potential, from advancing tea-based therapeutics to aiding in the development of precision agriculture for tea plantations. Future efforts could focus on integrating Internet of Things (IoT) devices for real-time composition analysis and blockchain technology for maintaining quality assurance in the tea supply chain.
